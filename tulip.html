Tulip Festival

The Skagit Valley Tulip Festival is a perfect destination for either a day trip or a longer stay. We are adjacent to I-5, 60 miles north of Seattle and 70 miles south of Vancouver, BC, Canada. You will need a map of the tulip field area to help you navigate, as the fields don't have addresses and their location changes every year due to crop rotation.

RoozenGaarde will also have its 5-acre garden ready to greet visitors—they hand-planted 1/2 million bulbs in creative designs ready for people to enjoy. In addition, RoozenGaarde/Washington Bulb Company has 350 acres of tulips in the ground, all getting ready to spring forth for the April Festival.

Of course, as tulip fans know, accompanying the spectacular blooms are a myriad of events and activities—there is truly something for everyone! A quilt show, art shows, the US Bank Parade, a fairy party, the Tulip Pedal, The Tulip Run, alpacas, PACCAR open house, the street fair and the Kiwanis Salmon Barbecue are are planned and ready to go!

Also open to the public are our Key Bank Tulip Ambassador event in February and our gala opening (sponsored by Peoples Bank and Judd & Black Appliance) on March 29.

You can find out about all this and more right here on our website or give us a call.
